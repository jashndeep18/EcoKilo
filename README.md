🌱 EcoKilo – Turning Plastic into Profit

EcoKilo is a circular economy web platform that connects households, recyclers (kabadiwalas), and admins to convert plastic waste into money, rewards, and measurable environmental impact.

The platform enables users to submit plastic waste, schedule pickups, earn money & rewards, while recyclers manage pickups and admins monitor the entire ecosystem.

🚀 Key Objectives

Promote plastic recycling at the household level

Digitize the informal recycling ecosystem

Provide transparency in pricing, pickups, and payments

Track environmental impact (plastic saved, CO₂ reduced)

Enable a scalable, real-world circular economy model
